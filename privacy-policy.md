# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** April 17, 2026

## Introduction

Welcome to Stylescape ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Information You Provide
- **Account Information:** Email address, display name, profile photo
- **User Content:** Photos you upload (garment images, profile photos)
- **Feedback:** Comments, ratings, and feedback you submit
- **Communication:** Messages you send to our support team

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information
- **Usage Data:** Features used, time spent, interactions with the app
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **Performance Data:** App crashes, errors, and performance metrics
- **Analytics:** Anonymized usage patterns and trends

### 1.3 AI-Generated Content
- **Virtual Try-On Images:** AI-generated outfit visualizations created from your photos
- **Processing Metadata:** Technical data about image generation (quality scores, processing time)

**Important:** Virtual Try-On (VTON) images are processed temporarily for generation purposes only. We do not store, access, view, or sell VTON images. These images are generated on-demand and are yours to use, save, or share as you choose.

**Third-Party AI Processing:** Virtual try-on images are generated using Replicate's AI infrastructure. Your photos are transmitted securely to Replicate for processing and deleted within 1 hour. See Section 4.2 for details.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- **Provide Services:** Generate virtual try-on images, manage your wardrobe, create outfits
- **Improve AI Models:** Enhance the quality and accuracy of our virtual try-on technology for your experience only (we do not use your images to train models or for any other commercial purpose)
- **Personalization:** Recommend outfits and styles based on your preferences
- **Communication:** Send service updates, respond to inquiries, provide support
- **Analytics:** Understand usage patterns and improve app performance
- **Security:** Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents
- **Legal Compliance:** Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms

## 3. Data Retention and Deletion

### 3.1 User Content
- **Saved Outfits & Garments:** Retained until you delete them or close your account
- **Profile Photos:** Retained until you update or delete them
- **Temporary Processing Files:** Automatically deleted after 24 hours

### 3.2 Account Deletion
When you delete your account:
- All personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days
- All garment photos and saved outfits are removed from our servers
- VTON images you saved are deleted (any VTON images you downloaded to your device remain under your control)
- Anonymized analytics data may be retained for statistical purposes

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 4.1 We DO NOT Sell Your Data
We never sell your personal information to third parties. **This explicitly includes Virtual Try-On images—we do not sell, share, or use VTON images for any commercial purpose or data training.** VTON images are generated exclusively for your personal use and remain entirely in your control.

### 4.2 Service Providers
We share data with trusted service providers who help us operate:
- **Firebase/Google Cloud:** Hosting, authentication, database, storage
- **Replicate (AI Image Generation):** For virtual try-on features, we send your garment photos and profile photo to our AI partner, Replicate, to generate outfit visualizations. Your images are processed securely on Replicate's servers and automatically deleted within 1 hour. Replicate does not store, access, or use your images for any other purpose. [Replicate Privacy Policy](https://replicate.com/privacy)
- **Analytics:** Anonymized usage data for app improvement

### 4.3 Legal Requirements
We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to:
- Protect our legal rights
- Prevent fraud or security threats
- Comply with GDPR, CCPA, or other regulations

### 4.4 Business Transfers
If Stylescape is acquired or merged, your data may be transferred to the new entity.

## 5. Your Privacy Rights

### 5.1 GDPR Rights (EU Users)
- **Access:** Request a copy of your personal data
- **Rectification:** Correct inaccurate data
- **Erasure:** Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Portability:** Receive your data in a machine-readable format
- **Restriction:** Limit how we process your data
- **Objection:** Object to certain types of processing
- **Withdraw Consent:** Opt-out of data processing at any time

### 5.2 CCPA Rights (California Users)
- **Know:** What personal information we collect and how it's used
- **Delete:** Request deletion of your personal information
- **Opt-Out:** Opt-out of the "sale" of personal information (we don't sell data)
- **Non-Discrimination:** Equal service regardless of privacy choices

### 5.3 How to Exercise Your Rights
Contact us at: **stylescape.app@gmail.com**
We will respond within 30 days.
